About GSI

The Geological Survey of India (GSI) is one of the oldest organizations in the world and the second oldest in India. Established in 1851, GSI has grown into a repository of geo-science information through systematic geological mapping and mineral exploration. Today, GSI is an attached office to the Ministry of Mines with headquarters at Kolkata.
